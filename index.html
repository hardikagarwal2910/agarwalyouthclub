THE WHY

Ahmedabad holds one of India's largest Agarwal communities — and most of it has never met.

Tens of thousands of families, generations deep, sharing a name and a heritage. Yet walk into a samaj gathering and the faces are strangers. The community is immense; the connection is thin.

Agarwal Club Limited is the room that closes the distance — a curated circle where the next generation of Agarwal founders, professionals and families come to genuinely know one another. So that no member's child ever walks into a community hall knowing no one.
